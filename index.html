Welcome to Taniti

About Taniti

Taniti is a small tropical Pacific island. While the island is only about 500 square miles in size, the terrain is diverse, with sandy and rocky beaches, a small but secure harbor, lush tropical rainforests, and a hilly interior with a small, active volcano. Taniti has a population of roughly 20,000 indigenous people. Until recently, the majority of the Tanitian economy was controlled by fishing or agriculture.
